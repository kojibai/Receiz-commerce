# Code Of Conduct

Receiz Commerce Kit should be a useful place to build and learn.

## Standards

Expected behavior:

- Be direct, respectful, and specific.
- Focus technical criticism on code, docs, tests, security, and product behavior.
- Assume public comments may be read by new developers learning the SDK.
- Keep discussions grounded in reproducible behavior and clear tradeoffs.
- Respect maintainer decisions about proof authority, tenant isolation, and release quality.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information, secrets, identity artifacts, or credentials.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass security, proof, or release gates.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers redirect the thread.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Maintainers may also limit participation for contributors who repeatedly ignore project standards.

Security reports should follow `SECURITY.md`, not public issues.
